What is Life Transformation Coaching

Life Coaching is a powerful process during which individuals gain clarity on what they want in life and how to achieve it. As your Life Coach, I will show you that you already have all the resources you need inside of you to reach your goals. You will discover what's most important to you, and learn how to align your life to your values for maximum fulfilment. Whatever your goal, I will help bridge the gap between where you are now and where you want to be.

  • Discover your strengths, passions, dreams and purpose
  • Get unstuck any limiting beliefs, obstacles or fears
  • Regain your confidence and self-esteem
  • Have control over your emotions
  • Develop and enhance your relationships
  • Establish your goals and create an achievable action plan
  • Feel empowered to take control of your life
  • Acknowledge and celebrate all of your achievements
  • Love and believe in yourself.

What is Academic Life Coaching

The Academic Life Coaching (ALC) program is designed for students to thrive and develop positive life skills. The program is comprised of methods and concepts delivered by a trained Life Coach. The ALC Program goes beyond youth mentoring and counseling by helping teens understand how best to learn, be proactive, and exercise personal leadership. The skills teens learn in the present have an exponential impact on their future.

All boys and girls in the age group of 11 to 18 years, who want to discover their unique thinking, learning, and motivation styles.
